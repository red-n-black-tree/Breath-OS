Arc Tool

  • Draw Arc entities.

Tool Operation

  1. Click at starting point of arc.
  2. Move cursor.
  3. Click at ending point of arc.
  4. Move cursor perpendicular to line.
  5. Click to finish arc.
  6. Esc = Cancel operation.

Modifier Keys

  • None.
