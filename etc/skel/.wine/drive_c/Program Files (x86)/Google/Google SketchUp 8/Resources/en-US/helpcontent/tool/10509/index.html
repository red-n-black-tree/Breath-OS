Zoom Tool

  • Move camera (your view) in or out.

Tool Operation

  1. Click and hold anywhere in drawing area.
  2. Drag cursor up to zoom in (closer to model) and down to zoom out (farther from model).

Modifier Keys

  • Shift = Change field of view degrees
  • Esc = Enable previously selected tool

Advanced Operations

  • Zooming with a Scroll Wheel Mouse
  • Centering the Point of View
  • Changing Field of View
