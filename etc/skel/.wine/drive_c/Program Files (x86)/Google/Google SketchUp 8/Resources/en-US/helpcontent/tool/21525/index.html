Follow Me Tool

  • Duplicate a face along a path.

Tool Operation

  1. Identify edge of geometry to modify. This edge is the path.
  2. Draw a face perpendicular to path.
  3. Click on Follow Me tool.
  4. Click on face.
  5. Drag cursor to end of path.
  6. Click to complete Follow Me operation.
  7. Esc = Cancel operation.

Modifier Keys

  • Alt = Use perimeter of surface as the path

Advanced Operations

  • Automatically Extruding a Face Along a Single Surface Path
  • Creating a Lathed Shape
